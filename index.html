WHO WE ARE??

We are the guardians of Lahore's fast food history. Rangila Burger began with a simple cart and a dream.

40 years later, we are a Mall Road institution. We don't just flip burgers; we craft nostalgia. Our "desi-style" smash patties and secret spices have fed generations. From the chaos of the 80s to the modern era, our taste has never changed.

Authentic. Spicy. Rangila.
